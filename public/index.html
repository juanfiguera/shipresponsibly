Surgeon General's Warning

Vibecoding has been found to cause unreasonable confidence in first-time developers, leading to the deployment of untested software to unsuspecting humans.

Side effects may include: mass credential exposure, invoices sent to the wrong continent, and the mass psychological event known as "it works on my machine."
